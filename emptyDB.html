GENERAL INFORMATION MIAMI

IMPORTANT INFORMATION IN MIAMI
CURRENCY
The local currency is the US Dollar ($).You can exchange currency at Kahului Airport (OGG), or at resort destinations. Major credit cards are widely accepted and there are a number of ATM machines. Traveler's cheques are accepted at many establishments.

EMERGENCY ASSISTANCE
If you do require assistance within an emergency please contact one of the Winners Circle Events Team. Alternatively you can call the emergency services if required on: Tel. 911.

SMOKING
In accordance with the Hawaii Smoke-Free Law (Act 295), smoking is prohibited in enclosed and partially enclosed areas and within 20 feet of doorways, (including guest room lanais). There are designated smokingareas throughout the resort gardens by the thatched huts with ash urns as shown on the map opposite. If you have questions, please call the Grand Wailea Front Desk on extension 50 or contact a member of the Winners Team.

CIRCLE LOUNGE

RESTAURANT INFORMATION

FONTAINEBLEAU RESTAURANTS

 

BISTRO MOLOKINI
In the heart of the resort, enjoy a relaxed an open-air ambience with breathtaking views of the Pacific and distant islands. Featuring an exhibition kitchen and kiawe wood-burning oven, the Bistro offers a blend of California and Island cuisine.


Bar Open Hours: 11am - 9pm
Lunch Open Hours: 11am - 5.30pm
Dinner Open Hours: 5.30pm - 9pm

BISTRO BAR
A beachside bar offering stunning views of the Pacific and
neighbouring islands, specializing in Cocktails.

Open Hours: 11am - 9pm

BOTERO BAR
This bar is set amidst Hawaii's largest collection of art including the famous sculptures of Fernando Botero. This Maui bar offers live entertainment with some of the best Hawaiian music from 5pm - 9:30 pm.

Open Hours: 12.30pm - 12am

GROTTO BAR
Exclusive to Grand Wailea guests only, this swim-up bar is set amidst magnificent waterfalls, slides and grottos featuring delicious cocktails.

Open Hours: 12pm - 5pm


VOLCANO BAR
A poolside restaurant offering a fun and relaxed atmosphere to enjoy lunch and cocktails. Volcano Bar serves salads, sandwiches, freshly grilled hamburgers and fresh fish, and of course, exotic tropical drinks.

Lunch Open Hours: 11pm - 4pm
Bar Open Hours: 10pm - 5pm


HUMUHUMU
This ocean-side restaurant offers the most spectacular sunset views. Named after Hawaii's state fish, the Polynesian thatch roof restaurant seems to float on a million-gallon saltwater lagoon filled with tropical fish. Select your own lobster from the lagoon or savor delicious Island fish and meat entrees with Polynesian or Hawaiian influences.

Lounge Open Hours: 5pm - 9pm
Dinner Open Hours: 5.30pm - 9pm

WINNERS DIARY
ANOTHER GREAT WELCOME RECEPTION
THURSDAY 16TH APRIL 2013
ONSITE AT THE HOTEL

With yesterday's amazing Top Achiever Lunch on my mind, I wasn't sure what could possibly be in store for us. But as I followed the scent of an Italian feast wafting through the museum like lobby of the Cavalieri hotel, I knew instantly we were in for another wonderful experience.

The area was filled with smiles and laughter, the site of good friends and colleagues congratulating ourselves on another year well done! The reception looked amazing, spread through-out the marble foyer, into various rooms and spilling out into the poolside lounge. The lounge like setting outside provided a great opportunity to relax while catching up with old friends I haven't seen since the Bahamas (Winners Circle 2009). While the famous artwork and statues provided a nice back-ground to the many joyous conversations taking place on the inside.

As the Welcome Reception came to an end, we continued the party at the Circle Club. Great music and good friends - not a bad way to end the night.

I'm off to the Kick-off breakfast, where Kevin McCloud will be sharing his knowledge on the history and architecture of Rome - this will certainly provide great insight for me, and the Tours and Activities I have planned shortly after.
